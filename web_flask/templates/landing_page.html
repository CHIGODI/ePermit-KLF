FAQs

How do I apply for a permit online?

To apply for a permit online, you need to first create an account on our platform. Once logged in, navigate to the "Permits" section and follow the instructions to fill out the application form.

How can I track my permit application status?

You can track your permit application status by logging in to your account and navigating to the "Permits" section. There, you will find a list of all your submitted applications along with their current status.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, online bank transfers, and payment wallets. You can choose the most convenient option for you during the payment process.

Can I apply for multiple permits at once?

Yes, you can apply for multiple permits simultaneously. Simply select the permits you need from the available options and fill out the application forms accordingly.

How long does it take to process a permit application??

The processing time for a permit application varies depending on the type of permit and the volume of applications received. Typically, it takes between 3 to 5 business days for a standard permit application to be processed.

Can I cancel or modify my permit application after submission?

Once a permit application is submitted, it enters the processing stage, and changes or cancellations may not be possible. However, you can contact our support team for assistance and further guidance.
