FAQs

How do I apply for a permit online?

To apply for a permit online, you need to first create an account on our platform. Once logged in, navigate to the "Register business" section and fill out the application form.

How can I track my permit application status?

You can track your permit application status by logging in to your account and navigating to the "my businesses" section. There, you will find a list of all your submitted applications along with their current status.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mpesa payment method for now,

Can I apply for multiple businesses at once?

Yes, you can apply for multiple businesses simultaneously. Simply fill out the registartion form and after your business has been aproved you can go ahead to apply for permit

How long does it take to process a permit application??

The processing time for a permit application should be immidiatley permits will only be available for verified businesses

Can I cancel or modify my permit application after submission?

Once a business application is submitted, it enters the processing stage, and changes or cancellations may not be possible. However, you can contact our support team for assistance and further guidance.
