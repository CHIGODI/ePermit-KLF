About Us

ePermit was inspired by the need to simplify the process of obtaining business permits.We found a common interest in the business world and looking into that field, the registration process and acquiring of business permits seems so lengthy and complicated. with the evolving world of technology we decided to put our brains into test and design a platform to perform the tasks. Our team is dedicated to making this process as seamless as possible, ensuring that business owners can focus on what they do best. This project as part of our portfolio for Holberton School.
